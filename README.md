# 📊 FUTURE_DS_03 – Marketing Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
**FUTURE_DS_03** is a strategic data analytics project focused on optimizing marketing campaign efficiency. The objective was to analyze customer response data to identify high-conversion segments and improve the success rate of direct marketing initiatives.

By transforming raw banking and demographic data into a high-fidelity, "Cyberpunk" styled interactive dashboard, this project provides a data-driven framework for targeted marketing, helping to reduce acquisition costs and maximize campaign ROI.

## 📈 Key Business Insights & Strategic Analysis
* **Conversion Efficiency:** The campaign achieved an overall **Success Rate of 11.27%**, with 4,640 successful conversions out of a total audience of 41,188.
* **Professional Segment Trends:** My analysis identifies specific job sectors, such as **Admin and Technicians**, as the highest volume contributors to total audience, while highlighting performance gaps in other employment categories.
* **Temporal Performance:** Through the **Success Rate by Month** analysis, I identified significant fluctuations in campaign effectiveness throughout the year, providing a roadmap for better timing of future marketing spends.
* **Demographic Influence:** The dashboard reveals how education levels and housing status correlate with conversion likelihood, allowing for more granular audience targeting.

## 🧹 Data Preparation & Advanced Analysis
* **Data Engineering (ETL):** Utilized **Power Query** to clean and transform raw CSV data, ensuring correct data types for age, job, and marital status.
* **Advanced DAX Modeling:** Engineered custom measures including **Total Audience Count**, **Successful Conversions**, and **Success Rate %**.
* **Interactive Visualization:** Designed a custom dark-themed UI with neon aqua accents, featuring dynamic slicers and cross-filtering for deeper segment analysis.
